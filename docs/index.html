What is Arqitect?

Arqitect is the ecosystem for a self-evolving agent world. It hosts the modular organs — nerves, adapters, connectors, and tools — that let autonomous systems sense, act, and grow on their own.

01
Emerge

Nerves are synthesized and qualified automatically. The system evolves its own capabilities through use.

02
Compose

Tools, prompts, and test cases combine into modular behaviors. The system assembles what it needs.

03
Connect

Connectors bridge the system to the outside world — Discord, Slack, Telegram, WhatsApp, and more.

04
Contribute

Guardians contribute new senses, abilities, and extensions. The ecosystem validates and absorbs them.
